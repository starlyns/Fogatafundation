FAQ

Honest answers to fair questions.

What's the difference between a draft and validated Scripture?+

A draft is raw material — a fast first pass that gives translators a head start. Validated Scripture has been checked verse by verse by trained translators and the partner ministry, with the community it serves. We label the two clearly and never present a draft as finished Scripture.

What role does AI actually play?+

AI takes existing linguistic data from partner ministries and produces a first translation draft — work that once took years compressed into months. Then people take over: a missionary field-checks it with speakers, and the partner ministry validates it. AI accelerates; humans decide.

Who owns the translation data?+

The ministries that created it — always. Fogata never claims ownership of any linguistic data or translation. Partners share what they choose, on their terms, and can withdraw at any time. We are the connector, not the owner.
